About

Chendhuran College of Engineering and Technology was established by Thiruchendur Murugan Trust on 6th July 2006, with the noble vision to have professionals and leaders of high intellectual caliber and immaculate character, nurtured with a well-built motivation and obligation to serve humanity. The College was started as a private self-financing engineering college imparting knowledge in the areas of engineering, technology and education. The Institution under the vibrant leadership of Honorable Chairman, Sri. AVM. Selvaraj, has been intensifying development activities, to bring fruits of research for the betterment of the society, with the gifted support and priceless leadership of the members in the trust.
